English proficiency is vital for academic and professional success, serving as the primary language for international communication, education, and business. The IELTS exam, a key benchmark for assessing English proficiency, is crucial for academic purposes. Within the test, writing is often one of the most challenging and important skills to master.

There are many tools available to help students prepare for IELTS writing, including chatbots (e.g. ChatGPT, DeepSeek, etc.), websites (e.g. IELTS Liz, IELTS Buddy, etc.), and mobile apps (e.g. IELTS Prep, IELTS Test Pro, etc.). However, these tools have their limitations. While chatbots are useful for providing general feedback and tips, they often lack the ability to assess essays with the accuracy and consistency of official IELTS human graders. Automated Essay Scoring (AES) systems can deliver consistent scoring aligned with IELTS rubrics, but they typically lack personalized feedback and conversational guidance, which are essential for effective learning.

This is where C3IChat comes in.

英语能力对于学术和职业成功至关重要，它是国际交流、教育和商业的主要语言。 雅思考试作为评估英语水平的重要标准，在学术方面尤为关键。在整个考试中，写作往往是最具挑战性且最重要的技能之一。

目前有许多工具可以帮助学生备考雅思写作，包括聊天机器人（如 ChatGPT、DeepSeek 等）、网站（如 IELTS Liz、IELTS Buddy 等），以及移动应用程序（如 IELTS Prep、IELTS Test Pro 等）。 然而，这些工具也存在一定的局限性。聊天机器人虽然在提供一般性反馈和建议方面很有帮助，但通常无法像官方雅思考官那样，准确且一致地评分作文。 自动作文评分（AES）系统可以根据雅思评分标准提供一致性的评分，但通常缺乏个性化反馈和对话式指导，而这些对于高效学习是不可或缺的。

这正是 C3IChat 发挥作用的地方。

C3IChat (Conversational with Indirect Instruction for IELTS Chatbot) is an innovative, AI-powered chatbot designed to help students prepare for the IELTS Writing Test. It combines the dynamic feedback capabilities of conversational AI with the precision of AES systems to deliver personalized teaching sessions and accurate essay assessments based on official IELTS scoring criteria.

What truly sets C3IChat apart from other tools is its collaborative whiteboard feature. This feature allows the chatbot to retain the student's essay throughout the conversation, removing the need for users to repeatedly copy and paste their essays after each revision. More importantly, it enables the chatbot to interact directly with the essay in real time, highlighting areas for improvement and applying suggested corrections, simulating a live tutoring session with a real teacher. The result is a more interactive, efficient, and user-friendly learning experience.

C3IChat（Conversational with Indirect Instruction for IELTS Chatbot）是一款创新的、由人工智能驱动的聊天机器人，旨在帮助学生备考雅思写作考试。 它结合了对话式 AI 的动态反馈能力与自动作文评分（AES）系统的精准评分功能，能够根据官方雅思评分标准，提供个性化的教学体验和准确的作文评估。

真正使 C3IChat 与其他工具区别开来的，是其协作式白板功能。 该功能允许聊天机器人在整个对话过程中保留学生的作文，避免用户在每次修改后反复复制和粘贴文章。 更重要的是，它使机器人能够实时与作文进行交互，高亮指出待改进的部分并给出修改建议，从而模拟出一对一教师指导的教学场景。 最终，C3IChat 为学生带来了一种更加互动、高效、且用户友好的学习体验。

The Prediction Model project was part of a Kaggle competition that involved building a classification model to predict student outcomes, whether they would drop out, remain enrolled, or graduate. The dataset came from a real higher education institution with 3539 entries and included a range of features such as demographic information (age, gender, socio-economic status), academic performance (grades from the first two semesters), and behavioral and social data (attendance records, participation in extracurricular activities). The goal was to develop a model that could accurately predict student trajectories and uncover patterns behind academic success or attrition.

该预测模型项目是 Kaggle 比赛的一部分，任务是构建一个分类模型，用于预测学生的学习结果，即学生是否会退学、继续就读，或顺利毕业。 数据集来自一个真实的高等教育机构，共包含 3539 条记录，涵盖了多种特征，包括人口统计信息（如年龄、性别、社会经济状况）、学业表现（前两学期的成绩），以及行为和社交数据（出勤记录、课外活动参与情况）。 项目的目标是开发一个能够准确预测学生发展路径的模型，并发现影响学业成功或辍学的潜在模式。

My work on this project covered the entire data science pipeline. I began with exploratory data analysis (EDA) to understand the distribution and structure of the data. This included identifying and visualizing outliers, analyzing missing values, and examining feature-target correlations. These insights guided the decisions I made in the preprocessing and modeling stages. I also created meaningful visualizations to communicate findings clearly and support data-driven decisions.

我在该项目中承担了完整的数据科学流程。 我从探索性数据分析（EDA）入手，以了解数据的分布和结构。 这一步包括识别并可视化异常值、分析缺失数据，以及研究特征与目标变量之间的相关性。 这些洞察为我在数据预处理和建模阶段的决策提供了指导。 我还创建了具有意义的数据可视化图表，以清晰地传达分析结果，并支持数据驱动的决策过程。

In the data preprocessing phase, I performed a range of essential steps to prepare the dataset for machine learning. I engineered new features where appropriate, encoded categorical variables using techniques like one-hot and label encoding, and scaled numerical variables to ensure consistency across models. I also addressed class imbalances and cleaned noisy or inconsistent entries to ensure the integrity of the dataset.

在数据预处理阶段，我执行了一系列关键步骤，以将数据集准备好用于机器学习。 我在适当的地方进行了特征工程，使用独热编码（One-Hot Encoding）和标签编码（Label Encoding）等方法对分类变量进行编码，并对数值型变量进行了缩放处理，以确保不同模型之间的一致性。 此外，我还处理了类别不平衡问题，并清洗了噪声数据和不一致的数据记录，以确保数据集的完整性和可靠性。

For model development, I experimented with several classification algorithms, including Random Forest, LightGBM, CatBoost, and Logistic Regression. I evaluated each model's performance using accuracy scores and error metrics, and I analyzed confusion matrices to better understand their strengths and weaknesses. After hyperparameter tuning and model comparison, I selected Random Forest as the final model, which achieved an accuracy of 80% in the Kaggle competition.

在模型开发阶段，我尝试了多种分类算法，包括随机森林（Random Forest）、LightGBM、CatBoost 以及逻辑回归（Logistic Regression）。 我使用准确率和误差指标对每个模型的性能进行了评估，并通过分析混淆矩阵，更深入地了解各模型的优劣势。 在完成超参数调优和模型对比后，我选择了随机森林作为最终模型，并在 Kaggle 比赛中达到了 80% 的准确率。
